## Who He Is

Co-founder + former Chief Scientist of OpenAI. Co-author of AlexNet (2012) and Seq2Seq (2014) — two of the most important papers in deep learning history. Hinton student. Departed OpenAI mid-2024 → founded **Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI)** — single-product company with no revenue, no products, only research toward safe ASI.

---

## NeurIPS 2024: The End of an Era

The talk that crystallized 2025's strategic pivot in AI.

### Central Thesis

> "Pre-training, as we know it, will unquestionably end."

### Peak Data Argument

> "Data is the fossil fuel of AI."

- We've reached **peak data**: the internet is humanity's only sufficiently large dataset, and it's finite
- "Computational power grows through better hardware and algorithms, but the data is not growing because we have but one internet"
- Web-scale datasets: duplication, bias, diminishing returns

### What Comes Next

| Path | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Synthetic data** | AI generates its own training material |
| **Reasoning + agentic AI** | Beyond pattern-matching → genuine reasoning + autonomous action |
| **Self-improvement** | Models that learn dynamically, not just from pre-training |

(Aligns directly with Karpathy's RLVR thesis → [[karpathy]])

## Future Superintelligence — Four Attributes

1. **Autonomous decision-making** — minimal human intervention
2. **Advanced reasoning** — surpassing human speed + accuracy
3. **Genuine understanding** — beyond statistical correlations
4. **Self-awareness** — introspective processing

> "Future systems will be more unpredictable" and harder to control.
