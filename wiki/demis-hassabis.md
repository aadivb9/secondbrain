## Who He Is

Co-founder + CEO of Google DeepMind. **Nobel laureate (Chemistry, with John Jumper) for AlphaFold** — predicting 3D protein structure. Former chess prodigy + neuroscientist + game designer. Bridges science + AI more credibly than anyone else in the field.

## AGI Stance

- **5–10 years away** from AGI
- 50% probability of AGI by 2030
- **Rejects** that AGI exists today
- **Hassabis test for AGI:** Could the AI independently derive Einstein's relativity from only pre-1900 knowledge? (Generative scientific reasoning, not interpolation.)

## Two Steps Required for AGI

> Per Dec 2025 podcast.

1. **World model** — AI must truly understand physics + space
2. **Automated experimentation** — AI can ask questions, verify, iterate on its own

These must connect into a complete scientific research closed-loop.

## Diagnosis of Current AI

> Current AI is **"jagged"** and **"frozen."**

- Solves graduate-level problems then fails basic logic
- Frozen models lack flexibility for true AGI
- "True AGI requires more than scaling text prediction. It demands understanding the physical world."
