## Who He Is

Turing Award winner (2018, with Hinton + Bengio). Inventor of CNNs (1989) → backbone of computer vision. **Former** Chief AI Scientist at Meta (2013–Nov 2025). Founded **AMI Labs** (Advanced Machine Intelligence) March 2026 — **$1.03B seed at $3.5B valuation**, Paris HQ. Most prominent contrarian against the LLM-scaling consensus.

---

## The Bet Against LLMs

> "The path to superintelligence through scaling LLMs is **complete bullshit**."

> "LLMs are now technology development, not research."

> To academics: "Don't work on LLMs. There is no point. You're not going to be able to rival what's going on in industry."

**Prediction:** LLMs **obsolete in 5 years**.

**Critique:**
- LLMs manipulate language well but **lack understanding of the physical world**
- LLMs perform poorly at images/video — "they really do not work"

---

## JEPA — Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture

LeCun's alternative architecture.

**Core idea:** Train AI to learn **abstract representations** of the world and make predictions in that abstract space — ignoring unpredictable details.

> "Learns underlying rules of the world from observation, like a baby learning about gravity."

**Trained from:** video, audio, sensor data — not text.

**Variants:** I-JEPA (images), V-JEPA (video), V-JEPA2.
