## TOP 5 CHANGES — April 2026 Update

Synthesized from recent CEO statements (Altman, Amodei, Huang, Hassabis, Nadella). Two tracks: **changes for Claude** (how I should work with you) and **changes for your life**.

### For Claude (how I'll work differently with you)

| # | Change | Why |
|---|--------|-----|

| # | Change | Exact action |

| 1 | **Master AI as a core tool — this is the new coding** | Use AI for SAT prep, lockedIn dev, school projects, volleyball film analysis. Treat fluency as a skill to level up daily. |

## THE ACTION LIST

Concrete things to implement, grouped by life area.

### 🎯 Personal Operating System

**From Altman's *How to Be Successful*:**
- [ ] **No zero days.** Code, study, or train — every day. Compounding principle #1.
- [ ] **Asymmetric bets now.** You're 15 — lowest opportunity cost of your life. Take risks you can't take later.
- [ ] **Pick one hard thing.** Focus until dominant, then next. Don't start lockedIn v2 until v1 ships.
- [ ] **Own things early.** Equity in even tiny projects > salary mentality.
- [ ] **Be hard to compete with:** software + design + volleyball + writing = rare stack. Defend this combo.
- [ ] **Self-belief + openness.** Share ambitious plans with mentors, not cynics.

**From Karpathy's learning heuristic:**
- [ ] **Depth-first projects, not breadth-first courses.** Finish a project before starting next.
- [ ] **Teach/summarize everything.** Create reel or wiki page for every big topic you learn.
- [ ] **Compare only to younger you.** Not to others.

**From Graham's *Life Is Short*:**
- [ ] **Schlep blindness check** — weekly: what am I unconsciously avoiding?
- [ ] **Maker schedule discipline** — protect 3+ hour blocks for hard work. Batch everything else.

### 💻 lockedIn (iOS App)

**From Tan's AI-era playbook:**
- [ ] **10% w/w growth target** — once launched. Post on your wall (Graham's rule).
- [ ] **Customer immersion** — spend 1 week using your own app + 3 friends' phones. Fix what hurts.
- [ ] **One metric:** pick either quiz pass rate OR retention. Ignore vanity.
- [ ] **Earnestness check** — are you building because you need it, or because it looks cool?

**From Altman's Playbook:**
- [ ] **Talk to users constantly.** After launch: text 10 users weekly, ask one question.
- [ ] **Launch faster than intuition suggests.** Cut scope until it's embarrassing.
- [ ] **Do things that don't scale** (→ Graham) — manually onboard each first user. FaceTime walkthroughs.
- [ ] **Founder mode** — don't outsource core product decisions. You ARE the product designer.

**From Amodei (AI economic shift):**
- [ ] **Bet on builders, not employees.** Continue building vs chasing internships.
- [ ] **Use Claude for everything** — it's what your target market will also use.

**From Jensen's anti-bundling:**
- [ ] **Don't bundle.** Block apps + quiz. Not block apps + quiz + social + journal + therapy.

**Pre-launch checklist (Graham's 13 sentences applied):**
- [ ] Launch reveals what to build → ship MVP April-May
- [ ] Idea evolves → v1 plan is wrong, that's fine
- [ ] Few users love it > many users like it → start with 10 fanatics
- [ ] Spend little → no paid ads, no tools subscriptions beyond Apple Dev
- [ ] Don't give up + morph → expect 3 pivots before finding it
