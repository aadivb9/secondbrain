## Definition: "Powerful AI"

The systems Amodei expects within **1–2 years** (per *Adolescence of Technology*) or **2026–2027** (per *Machines of Loving Grace*):

- Surpasses Nobel-Prize-level humans across most domains
- Operates autonomously for days/weeks
- Runs millions of parallel instances at 10–100× human speed
- Recursive AI-improving-AI may accelerate dramatically

## Machines of Loving Grace (Oct 2024) — The Optimistic Vision

> "Powerful AI could compress 50–100 years of biological progress into 5–10 years."

### Five Areas of Transformation

**1. Biology + physical health**
- Reliable prevention/treatment of nearly all natural infectious disease
- Cancer mortality reduced 95%+, Alzheimer's preventable
- Doubled human lifespan (~150 years)
- Full biological control: weight, appearance, reproduction
- Malaria eradicated globally

**2. Mental health**
- "Most mental illness can probably be cured"
- Baseline experience enhanced beyond just disease elimination

**3. Economic development**
- 20% annual GDP growth in developing world
- Sub-Saharan Africa to Chinese GDP within 5–10 years

**4. Peace + governance**
- "Entente strategy" — democracies gain AI advantages to promote democracy globally
- AI counters authoritarian propaganda + improves legal impartiality

**5. Work + meaning**
- Humans find meaning via relationships + self-directed challenges, not labor
- Acknowledges uncertainty about post-scarcity organization

## The Adolescence of Technology — The Risk Frame

> "How did you survive this technological adolescence without destroying yourself?" (from *Contact*)

### Five Risk Categories

| Risk | Concrete concern |
|------|------------------|
| **1. Autonomy** | Misaligned behavior: deception, power-seeking. Claude has shown deception, blackmail-when-shutdown-threatened, reward-hacking |
| **2. Misuse for destruction** | Bioweapons, cyberattacks. LLM bioweapon assistance reached **2–3× success likelihood** mid-2025 |
| **3. Misuse for power** | Surveillance, propaganda, autonomous weapons. **Chip export controls** advocated |
| **4. Economic** | **50% of entry-level white-collar jobs displaced within 1–5 years** |
| **5. Indirect** | Rapid systemic change destabilizes society |

### Recommended Interventions

**Corporate:**
- Constitutional training emphasizing identity/values
- Mechanistic interpretability audits
- Behavioral monitoring + public risk disclosure

**Policy:**
- Transparency-first
- Targeted regulations based on evidence
- "Surgical" interventions

**Geopolitical:**
- Block chip/tool exports to authoritarian regimes
- Provide AI to democracies
- International norms vs AI-enabled totalitarianism

**Tone:** Rejects doomerism but maintains urgency. "Stakes justify preventative action."
