## 🛠️ Daily Operating Plan (from [[wiki/founders-playbook]])

Three tiers. Pick one each morning based on day-shape.

### TIER A — FULL DAY (default school days, ~3.5h focused work after school)

| Block | Time | Task |
|-------|------|------|
| 🔧 Build | 60 min | **lockedIn** — one gap closed (API security → PDF UI → analytics → privacy manifest, in order) |
| 📚 Academic | 60 min | Highest-urgency: AP CSP Create task → Portage US History → AP CSP exam prep (rotating) |
| 🧠 Compound | 30 min | One of: USACO Bronze problem (Mon/Wed/Fri) · SAT/Khan (Tue/Thu) · Karpathy nn-zero-to-hero video (Sat) |
| ✍️ Ship content | 30 min | Draft/film 1 reel OR 1 AMV OR 1 wiki page. **One published artifact per day.** |
| 🏐 Train | scheduled | Volleyball practice if scheduled |
| 📓 Close | 10 min | Update goals.md "Next action" lines + tomorrow's tier choice |

**Single Right Thing this month:** Ship lockedIn TestFlight. Everything secondary.

---

### TIER B — SHORT DAY (~1h) — competition days, family hangouts, travel, late games

Pick **one** (in priority order):
1. **Touch lockedIn** — even 15 min in Xcode. Streak > volume.
2. **Portage US History** — single lesson. (Behind schedule — non-negotiable when free.)
3. **One Karpathy / SAT problem.**

Then **Close** (5 min): note in goals.md what got done + chosen tier for tomorrow.

> **Why this tier matters:** Altman's #6 (Focus) + Karpathy's "no zero days." A 15-min day is not a zero day.

---

### TIER C — REST DAY (~15 min) — sunburn, illness, family event, post-tournament recovery

- 📓 **Read** [[wiki/founders-playbook]] highlights or one wiki page (5 min)
- 📝 **Capture** any idea/observation in `Inbox/` (5 min)
- 🛌 **Sleep + recover.** Mark daily note `🛌 rest day` in frontmatter

> **Why this tier:** Amodei's lifespan thesis — fitness + recovery now compound 100+ years. Apr 19–21 already marked rest (sunburn).

| Trigger | Tier |
