# AI CEO Advice — 2025–2026 Synthesis

Raw aggregation of statements from Sam Altman, Dario Amodei, Demis Hassabis, Jensen Huang, Yann LeCun, Satya Nadella on personal development, careers, education, and the future.

---

## Sam Altman (OpenAI CEO)

- "The obvious tactical thing is just get really good at using AI tools. When I was graduating from high school, the obvious tactical thing was get really good at coding — this is the new version of that." (Fortune, March 2025)
- "Listening to old people is the biggest mistake young people make." (IIT Delhi speech)
- Gen Z using ChatGPT like a "life advisor" — Altman endorses this use pattern
- "Current jobs are going to get disrupted as AI can do more and more of the things that drive our economy today."
- Even the CEO role is not safe: Altman said AI will soon perform the work better than "certainly me" (Fortune, Feb 2026)
- Gen Alpha (in 10 years): graduates working in "solar system" jobs — entirely new categories (Fortune, Aug 2025)
- Encourages learn-how-to-learn over specific skills: resilience + adaptability > any one tool

## Jensen Huang (NVIDIA CEO)

- "You're not going to lose your job to an AI, but you're going to lose your job to someone who uses AI." (Milken Institute)
- "You're confusing your job with the tools you use to do it." — Workers scared of AI are confusing the PURPOSE of their job with the TASKS/TOOLS. (Fortune, April 2026)
- "You have to separate the task from the purpose of the job." (TechRadar, April 2026)
- Radiology paradox: AI predicted to kill radiology → instead, every platform became AI-driven → MORE radiologists, not fewer. AI lets them see more patients, diagnose faster.
- "Everyone should get an AI tutor right away — the tech should now teach humans." (Fortune, Feb 2025)
- AI agents will "harass and micromanage" workers — not take jobs. Employees will be busier. (Fortune, April 2026)
- At Davos 2026: nations should develop "Sovereign AI" — culture and language as natural resources
- AI will cause us to do MORE, not less (Jevons paradox view)

## Dario Amodei (Anthropic CEO)

- 50% of entry-level white-collar jobs at risk within 1–5 years (admin, consulting, financial services)
- "Powerful AI" within 1–2 years: surpasses Nobel-level humans, runs millions of instances at 10–100× human speed
- Lifespan could double to ~150 years via AI-accelerated biology
- AI can compress 50–100 years of biological progress into 5–10 years
- "Most mental illness can probably be cured" post-powerful-AI
- Cancer mortality reduced 95%+ within 10 years of AGI
- Spoke at India AI Summit Feb 2026 about serious risks: autonomous AI behavior, misuse, economic displacement
- Anthropic annual run rate crossed $30B (April 2026) — up from $9B end of 2025

## Demis Hassabis (Google DeepMind CEO)

- Nobel laureate (Chemistry 2024, AlphaFold)
- "AI will accelerate scientific productivity 10x faster than the Industrial Revolution"
- Forecasts AI will resolve most human diseases within 10–20 years via multimodal models
- AGI: 50% probability by 2030; full AGI 5–10 years
- First fully automated scientific lab launching in UK, 2026 (agreement Dec 2025)
- "Golden age of science" — AI as catalyst for discovery across drug dev, climate, materials, physics
- At Davos 2026: clashed with LeCun + Amodei on path to intelligent systems

## Satya Nadella (Microsoft CEO)

- AI is a platform shift comparable to the internet or mobile
- 2026 = "year of diffusion" — AI integrates deeply in the real world, not just in tech
- Warning: AI risks becoming an economic bubble if gains stay concentrated in tech firms rather than reshaping workflows broadly
- AI literacy — directing, managing, protecting AI — will become as fundamental as people management skills
- Serving as guest speaker for Stanford "Frontier Systems" course alongside Huang + Altman

## Yann LeCun (AMI Labs founder)

- Left Meta Nov 2025, founded AMI Labs March 2026: $1.03B seed @ $3.5B valuation, Paris HQ
- Maintains bet: LLMs are dead-end, world models are the only path
- LLMs "obsolete in 5 years"
- Positions AMI as third pole outside US-China binary
- "Breakthroughs will emerge from academia pursuing novel techniques"

## Cross-Figure Consensus on Young People / Education

1. **Master AI tools NOW** — Altman, Huang both say this explicitly. It's the new "learn to code."
2. **AI tutors for everyone** — Huang says get one immediately
3. **Separate job purpose from job tasks** — Huang's key framing
4. **Resilience + adaptability > specific skills** — Altman's meta-skill advice
5. **Builder/owner path** — Amodei's warning about entry-level jobs makes employee path risky
