# Demon Slayer: Key Life Lessons

## 1. No Weakness Cannot Be Overcome
Tanjiro spent 2 years training before splitting the boulder. Zenitsu went from cowardly to formidable. Persistent effort + refusal to accept defeat.

## 2. Teamwork > Solo Effort
Tanjiro's group + Hashira: diverse skills combined beat any solo power.

## 3. Show Grace Even to the Undeserving
Tanjiro listened to demons' stories and offered comfort before killing them. Understanding breeds mercy.

## 4. Stand Against Injustice
Love Hashira: "No way will I ever lose to evil people!" Creating better outcomes requires actively opposing unfairness.

## 5. Be Your Authentic Self
Mitsuri overcame bullying by accepting her unique traits. Diverse fighting styles flourish when embraced. Rengoku: "it's okay to do things a little differently!"

## Tanjiro's Breathing Arc

- Starts with Water Breathing: fluid, continuous, adaptive, defensive — ideal foundation
- Evolves to Hinokami Kagura / Sun Breathing: family ceremonial dance preserved for generations
- Water Breathing reflects Tanjiro's personality: kindness that adapts to protect

## Core Character Themes

Tanjiro = kindness + iron discipline. Trains for 2 years on one task without quitting. Feels grief for the very demons he must destroy.
