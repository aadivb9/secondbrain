# Short-Form Video Strategy 2026

## Content Structure Framework

1. Define goal first (awareness, growth, leads)
2. Know your audience via platform research
3. Choose 1–3 core platforms — don't spread thin
4. Hook in first 3 seconds
5. Film vertically (9:16), dynamic cuts, on-screen text
6. Always add captions — 50% of silent viewers rely on them
7. Authenticity over polish
8. Leverage trends only when aligned with brand
9. Post consistently — track and adapt

## Posting Frequency (2026)

| Platform | Minimum | Recommended |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| TikTok | 3x/week | 5–7x/week |
| Instagram Reels | 3x/week | 4–5x/week |
| YouTube Shorts | 2x/week | 3–5x/week |

## Optimal Length

- 31–60 seconds general
- Under 30 seconds often best for Instagram
- Quality 3x/week beats daily low-effort

## Format Winners

- Behind-the-scenes / day-in-the-life
- How-to tutorials and quick tips
- Product teasers / announcements
- Trending challenges with brand spin
- Q&A and myth-busting

## Key Metrics

Completion rate, engagement, follower growth, click-throughs, posting time patterns.
