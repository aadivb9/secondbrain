# Demis Hassabis — Raw Interview Extracts

Synthesized into [[wiki/demis-hassabis]].

## AGI Timeline & Definition

- Hassabis estimates **5–10 years away** from AGI
- 50% probability of AGI by 2030
- **Rejects** that AGI exists today
- **AGI test (Hassabis):** could AI independently derive Einstein's relativity from only pre-1900 knowledge?

## Two Key Steps to AGI (Dec 2025 podcast)

1. **World model** — AI must truly understand physics and space
2. **Automated experimentation** — AI can ask questions, verify, iterate on its own

These must be **connected** to form a complete scientific research closed-loop.

## Current AI Limitations

- Describes current AI as **"jagged"** and **"frozen"**
- "True AGI requires more than scaling text prediction. It demands understanding the physical world."
- Reaching superintelligence requires solving critical architectural gaps
- Frozen models lack flexibility for true AGI

## World Models / Genie

- DeepMind's Genie world models learn from vast video footage rather than hand-coded physics engines
- "If an AI can generate a world that behaves consistently, it must understand something meaningful about reality"
- Grounds intelligence in physical understanding rather than pure symbol manipulation

## Robotics

- Understanding spatial reasoning + cause-effect unlocks practical robotics
- Humanoid robots viable not for optimization but because the world is **fundamentally designed for human-shaped bodies**

## Active Projects (2026)

- **Dec 10, 2025:** DeepMind + UK government agreement to establish first fully automated scientific laboratory in 2026
- Collaboration with **Commonwealth Fusion Systems** to use AI to control plasma in tokamak reactor — last hurdle for fusion commercialization
- AlphaFold (with John Jumper) won Nobel — predicts 3D protein structure from amino acid sequence; released free

## Scientific Vision

- "Golden age of science" coming
- AI as catalyst for discovery: drug development, climate modeling, materials design, fundamental physics
- "Compressing decades of discovery into months"
- Augmenting rather than replacing human scientists

## Davos 2026

- Clashed with LeCun + Amodei publicly over the path to intelligent systems
