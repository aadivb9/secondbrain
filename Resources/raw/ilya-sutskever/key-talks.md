## NeurIPS 2024: "Pre-Training as We Know It Will End"

**Central thesis:** "Pre-training, as we know it, will unquestionably end."

### Peak Data Argument

> "Data is the fossil fuel of AI."

- We've reached **peak data**: the internet is humanity's only sufficiently large dataset, and it's finite
- "Computational power grows through better hardware and algorithms, but the data is not growing because we have but one internet"
- Likens situation to depletion of fossil fuels
- Web-scale datasets face finite utility due to duplication, bias, diminishing returns

### What Comes Next (3 emerging solutions)

1. **Synthetic data** — AI-generated training material
2. **Reasoning + agentic AI** — beyond pattern-matching toward genuine reasoning + autonomous action
3. **Self-improvement** — models that learn dynamically, not just from pre-training

### Future Superintelligence Attributes

- **Autonomous decision-making** with minimal human intervention
- **Advanced reasoning** surpassing human speed and accuracy
- **Genuine understanding** beyond statistical correlations
- **Self-awareness** enabling introspective processing

### Critical Challenges

- Alignment with human values
- Managing unpredictable autonomous behavior
- Societal disruption across economies + governance

> Future systems will be "more unpredictable" and harder to control.

### Biological Analogy

Mammalian brain evolution follows power-law distributions — incremental size increases yield disproportionate cognitive gains. Mirrors AI scaling hypothesis but emphasizes resource efficiency constraints.

## Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI)

- Founded after departure from OpenAI in 2024
- Focused exclusively on safe superintelligence
- Frames the transition to superintelligence as inevitable
- Requires careful development of safe, reasoning-capable AI
- No products, no commercial pressure — pure research approach
