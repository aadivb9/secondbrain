## Key Notes from Counselor
- Well-rounded applicant profile is lower risk than "pointy" — most colleges prefer depth + breadth over pure specialization
- Pointy only works if the spike is undeniable (national/international validation)
- Schools like CMU, Johns Hopkins, Caltech want niche deep passion with external proof
- Harvard wants well-rounded students who are still exceptional in their chosen field
- Penn values community impact locally, not "outsider savior" international projects
- Caltech: highly specialized STEM genius profile
- Harvard: broad excellence + strong domain achievement
